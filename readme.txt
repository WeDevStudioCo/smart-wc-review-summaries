Better WC Reviews enhances your WooCommerce store by using advanced AI technology to summarize product reviews.

== Description ==

Better WC Reviews is a plugin that enhances your WooCommerce store by using advanced AI technology to summarize product reviews. It helps potential customers get a quick understanding of the product feedback, improving their shopping experience and your conversion rates. The plugin also optimizes page load times by caching summaries, ensuring a smooth and fast user experience.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does the AI summarization work? =

The plugin uses advanced AI technology to generate a concise summary of the product reviews. It takes into account the sentiment and content of the reviews to provide a balanced and informative summary.

= Does this plugin affect page load times? =

No, the plugin optimizes page load times by caching the summaries. This means the AI does not need to generate a new summary every time the page is loaded.
